===Bus accident deaths involving Ahn Cheol-soo's campaigners===
On 15 February 2022, the first legal day of the presidential campaign, two members of the [[People Party (South Korea)|People's Party]] presidential campaign died in an apparent accident with the campaign tour bus. The bus driver and the campaign chair for the city of [[Nonsan]] died of apparent [[carbon monoxide poisoning]] inside the bus in the central city in [[Cheonan]] around 5:24 PM local time. One person was also rushed to a local hospital while unconscious. In response, Ahn halted all scheduled appearances for the remainder of the week.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=20220215000941|title=2 die in apparent accident in Ahn Cheol-soo's campaign bus|work=The Korea Herald|date=15 February 2022|access-date=9 March 2022}}</ref>
